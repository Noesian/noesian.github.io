# Calls to Action

Welcome to the `call` directory[cite: 1]. This section contains the primary outreach documents and invitations to participate in the Noesian movement, designed to mobilize individuals from passive secularism into active community building[cite: 1].

## Documents
* **[A Call to the Builders](call_to_the_builders.md)** – Directed at organizers, writers, artists, and creators. This document is a specific invitation to those ready to help construct the physical, cultural, and logistical infrastructure of the movement[cite: 1].
* **[The Noesian Call to You](noesian_call_to_you.md)** – A general, personal invitation to individuals seeking a secular, reality-based community. It serves as an introduction to our core pillars and a call to adopt radical agency, empirical mutual aid, and reclaimed ritual in everyday life[cite: 1].
